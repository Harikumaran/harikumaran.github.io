Savor a taste of the Southwest with delectable dining experiences at The Hotel-Ocean. The historic hotel boasts three unique restaurants and dining options, including an incredible open air, seasonal bar.

Regardless of which variety you choose, our creative New Mexican cuisine features only the freshest ingredients to create the most luscious meal for every occasion, from a quiet dinner or romantic date to weddings and anniversary celebrations.

Fresh and Flavorful New Mexican Cuisine

Guests seeking a unique restaurant will find satisfaction at The Hotel-Ocean, an elegant restaurant located on the hotel's original patio built in the 1920's. Open for breakfast, lunch and dinner, Our Hotel is a feast for the senses.
