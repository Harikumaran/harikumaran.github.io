AWARD-WINNING SPA EXPERIENCE IN THE HOTEL-OCEAN
Embark on your wellness journey in this tranquil Ocean Spa

As a luxury spa resort in UAE, Our spa is the recipient of several awards, including Leading Spa Resort by the World Travel Awards.
Experience tranquility and comfort like no other at the resort spa while you perfect your play at our tournament-standard golf course to unwind and rejuvenate by the Ocean’s idyllic coastline and lush greens.


Pamper yourself with holistic beauty treatments and century-old massage therapies, provided by our skilled team of master therapists.
