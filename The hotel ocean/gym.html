Workout Facilities

The Hotel-Ocean is equipped with an efficient fitness room and a heated outdoor pool, open year round, so you can keep in shape while dining out at all the wonderful restaurants. And, when done with your workout, you can relax in our hot tub, located next to the pool. All are accessible with your room key.

  • The hours of the pool are from 9am - 4pm.
  • The hot tub is open from 9am to 9pm.
  • The fitness room is open 24/7.
  • The fitness room features assorted free weights, a Concept II Rowing Machine, an Assault Fitness Air Bike, two Sprint CT850 Entertainment Treadmills, two Sprint CR800 Entertainment Ellipticals, a Sprint CR800 Semi-Recumbent Bike and a Spirit CIC800 Indoor Cycle. All are wi-fi enabled.
  • Please note that you have to briefly step outside by the pool to access the fitness room.
